Have a Nexus 7? Get the Chrome Beta

1 Feb, 2015 · Read in about 1 min · (62 Words)

The Chrome browser on Nexus 7 has never had the Close All Tabs function like its brother on the phone. This made no sense, was annoying, and dragged on forever. Fortunately, the end is near. You can now download Chrome version 40 via the Chrome Beta app on Google Play.

Close All Tabs is accessed by long-pressing the usual tab close X.
