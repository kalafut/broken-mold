tmux + iterm2

7 Jan, 2014 · Read in about 1 min · (60 Words)

Both tmux and iterm2 are great, but the default interaction will have iterm scrolling your entire tmux window off the screen which is probably not what you want. To use iterm’s scrolling within tmux, visit Preferences -> Profiles -> Terminal and check:

Save lines to scrollback when an app status bar is present

Thanks to Dan Carley for this tip.
